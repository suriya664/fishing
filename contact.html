FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance, especially during peak season (November-May). However, we often have last-minute availability, so don't hesitate to call even if your trip is just a few days away!

What if the weather is bad?

Your safety is our top priority. If the captain determines conditions are unsafe, we'll work with you to reschedule at no charge, or provide a full refund. We monitor weather closely and will contact you 24-48 hours before your trip if conditions look questionable.

Do I need any experience?

Absolutely not! Our captains and mates provide hands-on instruction for anglers of all skill levels. We've taught countless first-timers, and many have caught their personal best fish with us. Just bring enthusiasm and we'll handle the rest!

Can I bring my kids?

Yes! We love introducing young anglers to the sport. Our Inshore Lite package is perfect for families. We provide child-sized life jackets and can adjust our fishing style to keep kids engaged. We recommend ages 6+ for most trips.

What should I bring?

We provide all fishing equipment, licenses, and ice. You should bring: sunscreen, sunglasses, hat, soft-soled shoes, camera, and any food/drinks you'd like (we provide water). A cooler for your catch is helpful but not required.
