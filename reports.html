Capt. John's Morning Report

January 25, 2026 • Offshore - The Ledge

SAILFISH ARE RUNNING HOT!

"Incredible morning today at 'The Ledge'. We saw massive schools of baitfish and the Sailfish were right behind them. Hooked into 4 today, landed 3. The bite is definitely peak right now between 120-150ft. Water temp is perfect at 74°F and the current is moving nicely. If you've been thinking about a Sailfish trip, NOW is the time! Kite fishing with live goggle eyes is producing the best results."

Bait Used Live Goggle Eyes

Capt. Sarah's Inshore Update

January 24, 2026 • Inshore - Mangroves

SNOOK BITE IS ON FIRE

"What a day in the mangroves! The Snook are stacked up around the docks and absolutely crushing live pilchards. We landed 12 keeper-sized fish and released probably 20 more. The incoming tide in the morning is producing the best action. Water clarity is excellent and the fish are aggressive. Also picked up some nice Redfish and a couple of Trout. Perfect conditions for families and beginners - the fish are cooperating!"

Bait Used Live Pilchards

Capt. Mike's Tournament Report

January 23, 2026 • Offshore - Deep Drop

WAHOO SEASON IN FULL SWING

"High-speed trolling is producing trophy Wahoo right now. Caught a beautiful 65-pounder this morning along with several in the 40lb range. The fish are holding in 200-300ft of water along the reef edge. Blue/silver lures trolled at 12-15 knots are the ticket. Also picked up some nice Kingfish and a bonus Blackfin Tuna. Water is crystal clear and the bite window is early morning - first light to 10am. Don't miss this bite!"

Catches 4 Wahoo, 3 Kings
Technique High-Speed Troll
Depth 200-300ft
